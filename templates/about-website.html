About the BetterWays website!

An Overview

The Problem:

Lack of information of neighborhood residents have about their own neighborhoods. A place to complain and advocate for ideas.

The Solution:

Developing a website to host relevant information about the neighborhood, by users who contribute.

Our Audience:

Young Adults: a cohort that is dependent on the various aspects of neighborhood life

Requirements

Technical Requirements

We will need to store information that we get from the users such as their arts, their job requirement, their emergency announcements.

Functional Requirements

Our users should be able to sign up, log in, as well as posting issues pertaining to their community, and marking when they are resolved.
